Work With Us

Thank you for your interest in exploring a career opportunity with the AITMC-MCC. AITMC-MCC has grown exponentially since its inception and it continues to expand its scope and reach more and more youth. As a result we are now actively recruiting a number of positions. We are looking out for individuals who are dynamic, passionate and committed to working for the less fortunate and the underexposed. We need individuals that are enthused about working for the community and who are able to think creatively as well as diligently. We seek qualified and experienced candidates to help us build our vision and fulfill your ambitions. Please submit your updated resume at csr@aitmc.in
We appreciate your interest and insights into our mission to transform the lives of needy youth living in poverty through skill based training.

About us

The All India Technical and Management Council is one of India’s paramount advisory bodies. As a prestigious non-governmental membership based organization AITMC articulates the interests of industries, entrepreneurs, academic institutions and the youth of India.
