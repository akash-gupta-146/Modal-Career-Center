About us

About company

  • Who We are

    The All India Technical and Management Council is one of India’s paramount advisory bodies. As a prestigious non-governmental membership based organization AITMC articulates the interests of industries, entrepreneurs, academic institutions and the youth of India. AITMC plays a persuasive role in crafting and sustaining an environment in which employment and entrepreneurship both flourish. Through the facilitation of partnerships and with an extensive network of influential corporate, individual members, and academic institutions, AITMC provides the resource, knowledge and support that are vital for effective employment and entrepreneurship opportunities. The Ministry of Labor & Employment has launched the National Career Service (NCS) to enable aspiring youth to pursue right career choice according to their aptitude. The Ministry has approved All India Technical and Management Council to set up 1 Model Career Centre in Gurgaon. This initiative aims to provide a variety of employment related services with primary focus on Career Counseling. Model Career Centre will be reservoirs of skilled talent pool, where the Industry can tap in from, thus establishing a seamless employer-employee connect.

  • All India Technical and Management

    Model Career Centre, also known as AITMC-MCC addresses this endemic problem with a market-based approach that is sensitive to the socio-economic needs of marginalized youth in the 18-35 years age group. The ministry of Labor & Employment has approved AITMC-MCC as one of the model career centre to accomplish the objective. i.e. to help jobseekers connect with potential employers and skill providers. AITMC-MCC has set up a network of all stakeholders across India that attempts to bridge this widening divide between those who have access to opportunities and those who are increasingly being marginalized from the ‘new economy’ jobs. AITMC-MCC is an example of synchronous social action of various stakeholders, which aims at mainstreaming and ensuring economic security to youth who could be victimized by poverty. To enable them to gain a foothold in the competitive job market, AITMC-MCC helps the youth to acquire the required livelihood and soft skills in an environment of learning and mentoring that is responsive to the individual’s emotional and developmental needs.

Our Vision

The AITMC-Model Career Center strives to be the preeminent career center model for designing and delivering comprehensive, innovative, and inclusive career and employment services to 1 million youth by 2020.
