    • On-Campus Interviews

      Our Parent Organization, AITMC organizes training and skill development programs by sending industry experts to its various member institutions to increase the employability prospects for the youth and to ensure the skills of the students are in line with the industry requirements. They conduct regular training sessions at its Centres of Excellence for upgrading and honing of skills of its students. The aim is to enable the students to realize their career aspirations and goals and to assist industries in finding work force most suitable for their requirements. The skills attained enable our students to confidently participate in the world of business. They manage 27 Centres of Excellence across India. AITMC is working towards its goal of establishing 550 Centres of Excellence in 550 districts across 21 states in India, thereby paving a way to help the Indian economy grow. In these centres, students are taught about the latest technologies and equipped with skills to match industry requirements. AITMC-MCC facilitates On-campus interviews in the AITMC’s Centre of Excellences .To request on-campus interviews, Write to us at csr@aitmc.in or post your request on AITMC-MCC’s Job Portal.

    • Employer Partnership Programme

      Our Parent Organisation , AITMC has initiated Earn While Learn Programme . Under the EWL Program; AITMC proposes to establish 550 COE’s i.e. Centres of Excellence in AICTE Affiliated Educational Institutions to be opened up in 550 districts across India with 1 CoE to be setup in each district in association with Industry and Corporate Houses. These COE’s shall have the state of the art laboratories with equipments of world class standards and latest technology for imparting practical knowledge of the theoretical aspects learned in the classroom. With this Objective, AICTE has joined us in this revolution of bringing about a change in the rural economy and addressing the problems of rural to urban migration and providing meaningful and sustainable employment to youth. Under this programe, Industry can brand their products on campuses.This Employer Partnership Programme can be faciliatated by AITMC-MCC .

About us

The All India Technical and Management Council is one of India’s paramount advisory bodies. As a prestigious non-governmental membership based organization AITMC articulates the interests of industries, entrepreneurs, academic institutions and the youth of India.
