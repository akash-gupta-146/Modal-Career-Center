Our Approach

AITMC-MCC aims to Link Youth (Job seekers) with Jobs & Career Opportunities (Job Providers/Employers) through Counselling & Training. AITMC in partnership with its Industry partners is supporting this initiative of the Govt. of India by setting up a Model Career Centre in Gurugram.

The AITMC-MCC shall perform the following functions:

1. Identifying talent through mobilization (direct / job fair, etc.)
2. Mapping of competency of the candidates
3. Upgrading requisite skills of the candidates
4. Linking highly trained, pre-screened and employable candidates to the industry
5. Affecting Cost effective recruitment process for companies
6. Facilitation Centre for Apprenticeship program (solution for industry)

About us

The All India Technical and Management Council is one of India’s paramount advisory bodies. As a prestigious non-governmental membership based organization AITMC articulates the interests of industries, entrepreneurs, academic institutions and the youth of India.
